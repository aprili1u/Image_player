Questionnaire

In the following you will be shown a video clip, which displays one or multiple fish. In the case of multiple fish, please focus only on the individual marked with "A". You will be asked to describe every behavior individual A displays during the clip. It might also be the case that no discernible behavior occurs throughout the clip. To indicate when you see a behavior, please click on the screen and select the marker "beginning" at the time in the clip when a behavior starts. Click on the video again and select the marker "end" at the time in the clip when the behavior is over. The markers can be removed and changed in case you want to select a different time. Once you have identified the time frame during which a behavior occurs, you will be asked to name the behavior, and subsequently be asked questions about the behavior. Afterwards, you will be able to revisit your first naming of the behavior. Please select the answer that fits the behavior best. The following questions are referring to the time in the clip within the markers that set beginning and end of the behaviour. You can re-watch the video clips if you want to.
